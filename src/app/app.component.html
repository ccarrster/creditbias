Credit Application Justice Bias scoring and self reporting
Finstra - Hack to the future 2020

Why

Applying for credit is affected by racial biases. Shame the banks into providing a just system.

How it works

After applying for a loan, you submit anonymized data about the results.
Your identifying details will be removed from the data.
Part of your submission will include your ethnicity.
We compare all the submissions and display the lending agencies racial bias.
Displaying this information should help direct everyone to just lenders.
