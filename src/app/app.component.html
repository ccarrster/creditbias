Lender Racial Bias Rank

We call out racial injustice in the financial industry.

How it works

  • We compare loans from lenders using anonymous loan data.
  • A lender with 5 stars has no racial bias
  • A lender with 0 stars has a large amount of racial bias.

How can you help?

  • Give your business to the lenders who are more just.
  • Submit anonymized data from your loan application to help call out racial injustice.
